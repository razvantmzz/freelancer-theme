intro: Mahike was my Bachelor Thesis in Computer Science, written at West University of Timisoara, Romania. It focused on creating an easy way for people to find and explore hiking trails in romanian mountains. The application is a concept, not ready for real life scenarious. It offers a wide range of hiking trails, grouped my mountain regions, google maps integrations, point of interests around the trails and an S.O.S System.
my-role: Sole Developer of the app working under the coordination of my thesis professor.
tech-talk:
  - The app was developed in Android Studio using Java. Offers a wide integration with Google Cloud Services such as:&nbsp Google Maps, Google Places, Firebase Authentication, Realtime Database, Storage and Functions.
